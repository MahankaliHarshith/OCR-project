 Getting Started with Training

                    

                        

                            1
                            Upload labeled receipts — Scan or photograph receipts and add the correct product codes, names and quantities as ground truth.
                        

                        

                            2
                            Run a benchmark — Compare OCR output against your ground truth to measure accuracy (precision, recall, F1 score).
                        

                        

                            3
                            Auto-optimize — Let the system search for the best OCR parameters. Choose a strategy (Smart Tune or Grid Search) and metric to optimize.
                        

                        

                            4
                            Save & apply profiles — Save the best parameters as a named profile. Apply it for all future scans.
